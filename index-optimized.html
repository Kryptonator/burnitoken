What is BurniToken?

BurniToken is more than just a token—it's a promise for a deflationary future. At Burni's core is a mechanism that permanently removes tokens from circulation to potentially increase the value of remaining tokens.

The Secret of Token Burning

Imagine tokens being burned like logs in a magical fire. They disappear forever! This process, called "token burning," reduces the total supply of Burni tokens. Fewer tokens can mean each one becomes more valuable, similar to rare collectibles.

Burni's Promise: "Blackholed"

Burni is marked as "Blackholed: YES". This means that the maximum supply of Burni tokens is fixed and no new tokens can ever be created. It's like throwing away the key to the vault! This ensures a truly deflationary ecosystem.
