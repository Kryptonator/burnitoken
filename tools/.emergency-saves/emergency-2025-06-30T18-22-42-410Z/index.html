What is BURNI Token?

BURNI is a revolutionary deflationary cryptocurrency built on the ultra-fast and efficient XRP Ledger (XRPL). Our core mission is to create a sustainable and appreciating asset through a unique, automated token-burning mechanism.

  • Automatic Deflation: Every 3 days, 3% of the total supply is permanently burned, reducing the total number of tokens in circulation.
  • Smart Locking: An additional 2% is locked for ecosystem development, ensuring continuous innovation and growth.
  • Complete Transparency: All transactions, including burns and locks, are publicly verifiable on the XRPL blockchain.
